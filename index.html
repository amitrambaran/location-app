How to use:
  1. Allow location access in your web browser to view your current location.
  2. Drag and drop a text file with a latitude and longitude seperated by a comma. This is your dropped location.
  3. Your distance from the dropped location can now be seen. To change your initial location, reset it by clicking your new initial location on the map.
  4. This is your selected location, which can be dragged around anywhere on the map to update your distance from the dropped location.
